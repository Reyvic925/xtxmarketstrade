Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LuxYield and cryptocurrency investments.

Starting your investment journey with LuxYield is simple. First, create an account on our platform. Then, deposit funds using one of our supported cryptocurrencies. Once your account is funded, select an investment plan that suits your goals and budget, then start earning returns immediately.

LuxYield currently accepts Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Tether (USDT), USD Coin (USDC), Binance USD (BUSD), and Solana (SOL) for deposits and investments. We may add support for additional cryptocurrencies in the future.

LuxYield employs a multi-layered security approach including cold storage for the majority of funds, two-factor authentication (2FA), and regular security audits. Our investment strategies are designed with risk management protocols to protect your capital.

Withdrawal fees vary depending on the cryptocurrency and network conditions. A small network fee may apply. Minimum withdrawal limits are in place to ensure efficient transaction processing. Please refer to our detailed fee schedule in your dashboard for precise figures.

Our investment plans are structured for specific durations to maximize compounding returns. Early withdrawal may be possible but could be subject to certain conditions or a penalty fee. We encourage users to carefully review the terms of each plan before investing.
